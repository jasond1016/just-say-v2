Final surface set

Four surfaces matter.

The product resolves into four distinct surfaces: a transcript-first Live Session workspace for long attention, a highly compressed PTT HUD that should barely interrupt the user at all, a searchable History archive for finding and reusing past speech, and a calm Settings surface for defaults that should mostly disappear from daily use.
