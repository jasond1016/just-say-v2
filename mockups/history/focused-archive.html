Overview
The redesign direction is now transcript-first.
The discussion converged on a quieter Live Session surface, a HUD-native Quick Dictation model, and a History flow that behaves more like opening documents than managing records in place.
Decisions
  • Live Session should keep transcript on one canvas from streaming through completion.
  • Quick Dictation should not rely on a page-first experience. The HUD is the primary surface.
  • History should separate retrieval from reading instead of forcing split view.
Action Items
  • Move low-frequency transcript actions under More so the header stops behaving like a toolbar.
  • Keep generated notes inside the detail page instead of branching into a separate side panel or modal.
  • Decide later whether generated notes should remain read-only or support lightweight inline edits.
